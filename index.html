About

Folk World is an online radio platform dedicated to Black music across all genres, eras, and worlds — a living archive, always on.

We broadcast the full breadth of the tradition: blues, jazz, gospel, soul, funk, hip-hop, afrobeat, folk, and everything in between. Music inseparable from its history.
