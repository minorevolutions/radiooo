About

Folk World is an online radio platform dedicated to Black music across all genres, eras, and worlds — from archival field recordings and rare interviews to live DJ sets and contemporary sound.

We broadcast the full breadth of the tradition: blues, jazz, gospel, soul, funk, hip-hop, afrobeat, folk, and everything in between. A living archive, always on.
